#GAStat - Google Analytics on iOS GAStat is an iOS app that lets you view your Google Analytics data and access key insights through home screen widgets. The app connects securely to your Google Analytics account and does not store your analytics data on any external servers. ## Key Features - **Real-time Analytics Access:** View your Google Analytics data directly from your iPhone or iPad - **Home Screen Widgets:** Get quick insights at a glance without opening the app - **No Data Storage:** Your analytics data is never stored on external servers ## How It Works 1. Sign in securely with your Google Account 2. Authorize access to your Google Analytics properties 3. View your data and customize your widgets ## Privacy & Security Your privacy is our priority. GAStat only accesses the Google Analytics data you explicitly authorize and does not retain or share your information with third parties. All connections are encrypted and secure.
